This hack was submitted to Music Hack Day Boston, 2013. Special thanks to the libraries that made this doable in a weekend:
  • cm-chessboard - used to render the board and pieces (no dependencies!).
  • chess.js - used to track game state.
  • Tonal.js - used to convert notes into frequencies.
  • Wikipedia - for the pictures of the chess players.

The samples used come from freesound.org and are:
  • bass synth 2 octave used under the Creative Commons 0 license.
  • Spanish Guitar Notes by pitx used under the Creative Commons Attribution - Non-commercial license.
