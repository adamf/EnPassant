About

En Passant turns a chess game into music. The sequencer sweeps across the files a–h; each occupied square becomes a note in an E-minor pentatonic scale, mid-register for White and low-register for Black.

Click a portrait to hear a famous game, or load your own via the ♪ menu.

By Adam Fletcher · GitHub · Music Hack Day Boston, 2013
